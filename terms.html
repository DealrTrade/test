DealrTrade is an online marketplace that connects private vehicle owners (Customers) with licensed franchise dealerships (Dealers) in Canada. To use the Platform as a Customer, you must be at least 18 years of age, a resident of Canada, and the registered owner (or authorized legal representative of the owner) of the vehicle you list. You must provide accurate, complete, and truthful information during registration and listing creation.

4.2 Vehicle Listings

When creating a Listing, you represent and warrant that:

  • All vehicle information provided is accurate and complete to the best of your knowledge.
  • You are the lawful owner or have full legal authority to list the vehicle for sale or trade.
  • The vehicle is free of undisclosed encumbrances, liens, security interests, or legal disputes preventing clean title transfer.

You are solely responsible for the accuracy of your Listing and any subsequent negotiations or transactions with Dealers. DealrTrade is not a party to any transaction between you and a Dealer and accepts no liability for the outcome of any such transaction.
