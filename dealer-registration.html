Application Summary
Dealer Platform Agreement — Summary of Key Terms

1. Platform Access & Eligibility

DealrTrade is a private B2B marketplace accessible only to licensed franchise automobile dealerships holding a valid provincial motor vehicle dealer registration. By submitting this application, you warrant that your dealership is currently licensed and in good standing with the applicable provincial regulator. DealrTrade reserves the right to verify registration at any time and to suspend or terminate access if registration lapses or is revoked.

2. Free Trial Period

New accounts receive an initial free trial period of 30 (thirty) days from the date of account activation. During this period, no subscription fees are charged. DealrTrade reserves the right to modify the duration or terms of the free trial at its sole discretion for future applicants. This free period is offered once per dealership and is not transferable.

3. Subscription & Automatic Renewal

Upon expiry of the free trial, your account will automatically convert to a paid monthly subscription at the then-current rate (presently $299/month + applicable HST). Your designated payment method will be charged on the same date each month. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled in writing. Cancellation must be received by DealrTrade at least 24 hours before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the following month. No partial-month refunds are provided.

4. Pricing & Rate Changes

DealrTrade's subscription rates are subject to change. We will provide a minimum of 30 (thirty) days written notice by email to the billing email address on file before any rate increase takes effect. Continued use of the platform following the effective date of a rate change constitutes your acceptance of the revised pricing. Failure to receive notice due to an invalid or outdated billing email address does not exempt the dealer from the new rate. It is the dealer's responsibility to maintain a current and valid billing email address.

5. Payment & Taxes

All fees are quoted in Canadian dollars (CAD). Applicable federal and provincial taxes (HST/GST/QST) will be added to each invoice based on your dealership's registered province. You agree to provide a valid credit card for recurring billing. In the event of a failed payment, DealrTrade will make two (2) retry attempts within 7 days. If payment remains outstanding after retry, your account may be suspended until payment is received. Outstanding balances accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% per annum).

6. Use of Platform & Data

Dealers agree to use DealrTrade solely for legitimate trade-in and vehicle purchase offers. Submission of fraudulent, misleading, or bad-faith offers is prohibited and may result in immediate account termination without refund. Dealer offer data (amounts, response rates, deal outcomes) is used by DealrTrade in aggregate and anonymized form for analytics, market reporting, and platform improvement. Individual dealer identity is not disclosed to customers during the offer period.

7. Deal Outcome Reporting

Dealers who appear in the top-three offers for any listing are required to report the deal outcome (completed, not completed, or in negotiation) within 30 days of the offer period closing. Failure to submit outcome reports may affect your dealer performance score, your ranking priority in future listings, or your continued access to the platform.

8. Confidentiality

Dealers agree to maintain the confidentiality of customer personal information received through the platform. Customer contact details shared upon offer period close may only be used for the purpose of completing the vehicle transaction and not for any other marketing or solicitation purpose without the customer's explicit consent. Misuse of customer data is grounds for immediate termination and may expose the dealer to liability under applicable privacy legislation including PIPEDA and provincial equivalents.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. DealrTrade reserves the right to immediately suspend or terminate access without notice in cases of: (a) breach of these terms; (b) fraudulent activity; (c) lapse of dealer registration; (d) non-payment; or (e) conduct that DealrTrade determines, in its sole discretion, to be harmful to the platform, its customers, or other dealers. No refund of subscription fees already paid will be provided upon termination for cause.

10. Limitation of Liability

DealrTrade provides its platform on an "as is" and "as available" basis. DealrTrade does not guarantee the number, quality, or value of offers received by any customer. DealrTrade is not a party to any transaction between dealers and customers and accepts no liability for the outcome of any vehicle purchase, trade-in, or negotiation facilitated through the platform. In no event shall DealrTrade's total liability to a dealer exceed the amount paid by that dealer in the 3 months preceding the claim.

11. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the federal laws of Canada applicable therein. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Ontario, Canada.

12. Entire Agreement

This application, together with DealrTrade's full Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy (available at dealrtrade.ca/terms), constitutes the entire agreement between the dealer and DealrTrade Inc. with respect to platform access. DealrTrade reserves the right to update these terms and will provide notice in accordance with Section 4 above.
